Data & Methodology

Our project uses a rich dataset of historical Dutch texts, including newspapers, magazines, and books spanning from World War II to the present day. The data is chronologically ordered and processed using the BERT infrastructure to create specialized models for analyzing conceptual change over time.

We focus on tracking the evolution of words and concepts related to sustainability, allowing us to explore shifts in public discourse on one of today's most critical global issues.
