About the Project

The Semantics of Sustainability project is funded by the NL eScience Center and aims to explore conceptual change in sustainability language over time using machine learning techniques.

The project utilizes historical Dutch textual data from World War II to the present, leveraging the BERT infrastructure to uncover how words related to sustainability have evolved.

Collaborators: eScience Center, Utrecht University, National Library The Hague.
